About

§

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Aldermont Institute of Technology, where I lead the Physical Intelligence Lab. My research develops machine learning for physical simulation and reinforcement learning — building differentiable simulators and agents that learn to model, predict, and control complex physical systems.

I am also drawn to the physics of antimatter, where I develop machine-learned models of matter–antimatter interactions.

I am recruiting PhD students for Fall 2027.
